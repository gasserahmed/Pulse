Description
------------
'Pulse' is an application that helps in keeping your body healthy and fit by providing people with different types of workouts and exercises.
It also provides people with symptoms of common sports injuries and methods to treat them.
Finally, stopwatch and countdown timer are included in this application with awesome features.
  

Features
---------
-Various workouts and exercises
-Common sports injuries and their treatments
-Stopwatch
-Countdown timer
-Elegant UI
